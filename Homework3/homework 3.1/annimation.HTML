Instructions

This assignment should be solved with animation and without touching the HTML. Experiment with different solutions and read about them on the web. Some solutions are more performant than others and will look more smooth

Don't overwrite the base CSS. Write your own CSS at the bottom of the stylesheet, after the comment.

  1. The animation should not start until 4 seconds after the page loads
  2. The ball should move in a square around the page.
  3. The ball should do this move three times clockwise/counterclockwise/clockwise.
  4. Every lap should take 4 seconds.
  5. You should set a timing-function that is not the default.

Tip:

You can use transform to move stuff around and scale them more freely.
